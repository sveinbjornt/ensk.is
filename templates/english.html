About ensk.is

ensk.is is a free and open public domain online English-Icelandic dictionary.

The author of the original dictionary, Geir T. Zoëga (1857-1928), was an English language teacher and later rector at the Learned School of Reykjavík in the years 1883-1927. The first edition was published in 1896. The digitised, extended and sanitised version presented here is based on the much-improved third edition (1932) edited by Þorsteinn Þorsteinsson.

This new edition of the dictionary has been comprehensively updated and modernised. The text now conforms to modern Icelandic orthography, various errors in the original have been corrected, and the vocabulary has been significantly expanded to reflect contemporary usage.

Ensk.is provides an easy-to-use JSON API. However, for bulk processing or low-latency applications, the downloadable version is recommended.

The current editor and maintainer is Sveinbjörn Þórðarson.
